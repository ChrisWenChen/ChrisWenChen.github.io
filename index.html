Wen Chen / 陈文

Ph.D. Student in Physics, University of Houston 休斯顿大学 物理学博士生

I am a Ph.D. student in physics at the University of Houston, working with Prof. Rubem Mondaini. My current research focuses on the sign problem in Quantum Monte Carlo methods. I previously completed my master’s degree at the Beijing Computational Science Research Center (CSRC), where I worked on determinant Quantum Monte Carlo and random matrix theory. My research interests also include analytical and numerical methods for quantum many-body systems, applications of random matrix theory in physics, Monte Carlo simulations in lattice quantum chromodynamics, and the use of AI agents in physics research.

我是休斯顿大学物理学博士生，导师为 Rubem Mondaini 教授。目前的研究方向为量子蒙特卡洛方法中的符号问题。此前在北京计算科学研究中心攻读硕士，研究行列式量子蒙特卡洛方法与随机矩阵理论。研究兴趣还包括量子多体系统的解析和数值方法、随机矩阵理论在物理学中的应用、格点量子色动力学的蒙特卡洛模拟、智能体在物理学研究中的应用等。
