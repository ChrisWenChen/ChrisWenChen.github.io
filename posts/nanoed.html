nanoED: Exact Diagonalization in ~60 Lines of Python nanoED：用不到 60 行 Python 做精确对角化

Wen Chen | Apr 7, 2026

Exact diagonalization (ED) is the most direct numerical method in quantum many-body physics: write the Hamiltonian as a matrix, diagonalize it, and obtain exact eigenvalues and eigenstates. It involves no physical approximations — the only limitation is that the matrix size grows exponentially with the number of sites, known as the “exponential wall.”

精确对角化（Exact Diagonalization, ED）是量子多体物理中最直接的数值方法：把哈密顿量写成矩阵，对角化，得到精确的本征值和本征态。它没有任何物理近似——唯一的限制是矩阵的大小随格点数指数增长，这被称为"指数墙"（exponential wall）。

This article implements a complete ED program nanoED in ~60 lines of Python, covering the full pipeline from Hilbert space construction to finite-size scaling. After running the code, the reader should obtain numerical results consistent with the Bethe ansatz exact solution, and gain an intuitive sense of the exponential wall's impact.

本文用不到 60 行 Python 实现一个完整的 ED 程序 nanoED，覆盖从 Hilbert 空间构造到有限尺寸标度的全部流程。读者运行代码后应能得到与 Bethe ansatz 精确解一致的数值结果，并直观感受到指数墙的威力。

1.1 Model: The Antiferromagnetic Heisenberg Chain 1.1 模型：反铁磁 Heisenberg 链

We choose the classic benchmark model for ED: the one-dimensional spin-1/2 antiferromagnetic Heisenberg chain with periodic boundary conditions (PBC):

我们选择最经典的 ED 基准模型：一维 spin-1/2 反铁磁 Heisenberg 链，周期边界条件（PBC）：

$$H = J \sum_{i=0}^{L-1} \mathbf{S}_i \cdot \mathbf{S}_{i+1} = J \sum_{i=0}^{L-1} \left[ S_i^z S_{i+1}^z + \frac{1}{2}\left(S_i^+ S_{i+1}^- + S_i^- S_{i+1}^+\right) \right]$$

where $S_{L} \equiv S_0$ (periodic boundary) and $J = 1$ (antiferromagnetic).

其中 $S_{L} \equiv S_0$（周期边界），$J = 1$（反铁磁）。

Why this model:

选择它的理由：

  • It is one of the most fundamental quantum many-body models in condensed matter physics
  • Bethe (1931) provided the exact solution; the ground-state energy density in the thermodynamic limit is $E_0/L = 1/4 - \ln 2 \approx -0.4431$
  • The natural correspondence between spin states and bits makes it ideally suited to bitwise operations and keeps the implementation especially compact
  • 它是凝聚态物理最基本的量子多体模型之一
  • Bethe (1931) 给出了精确解，基态能量密度在热力学极限为 $E_0/L = 1/4 - \ln 2 \approx -0.4431$
  • 自旋态与比特的对应关系天然适合位操作，编程最简洁

1.2 Binary Encoding: Spins as Bits 1.2 二进制编码：自旋即比特

A spin-1/2 system on $L$ sites has two states per site, $|\uparrow\rangle$ and $|\downarrow\rangle$. A many-body state is a tensor product of all $L$ sites, giving $2^L$ configurations in total. We label each configuration with an $L$-bit integer:

一个 $L$ 格点的 spin-1/2 系统，每个格点有两个状态 $|\uparrow\rangle$ 和 $|\downarrow\rangle$。一个多体态是 $L$ 个格点的直积，共 $2^L$ 种。我们用一个 $L$-bit 整数来标记它：

$$|s\rangle = |s_{L-1}, \ldots, s_1, s_0\rangle, \quad s_i \in \{0, 1\}$$

Bit $i$ being 1 means spin-up $|\uparrow\rangle$ at site $i$; 0 means spin-down $|\downarrow\rangle$. Note the distinction between the bit variable $s_i \in \{0, 1\}$ (used for encoding) and the physical spin eigenvalue $S_i^z = s_i - 1/2 \in \{-1/2, +1/2\}$ — both appear in the formulas below. For example, with $L=4$, the integer $10 = 1010_2$ represents spins up at sites 1 and 3, and down at sites 0 and 2.

bit $i$ 为 1 代表第 $i$ 个格点自旋向上 $|\uparrow\rangle$，为 0 代表向下 $|\downarrow\rangle$。注意区分位变量 $s_i \in \{0, 1\}$（编码用）和物理自旋本征值 $S_i^z = s_i - 1/2 \in \{-1/2, +1/2\}$——后文公式中两者都会出现。例如 $L=4$ 时，整数 $10 = 1010_2$ 表示第 1、3 格点向上，第 0、2 格点向下。

The advantage of this encoding: physical operators map directly to bit operations — $S_i^z$ reads bit $i$, $S_i^+ S_j^-$ flips two bits — no additional data structures needed.

这个编码的好处是：物理算符直接对应位操作——$S_i^z$ 读第 $i$ 位，$S_i^+ S_j^-$ 翻转两位，不需要任何额外数据结构。

Reducing the dimension with conserved quantities. The Heisenberg Hamiltonian conserves the total magnetization $S^z_\text{tot} = \sum_i S_i^z$. Why is it conserved? We can check term by term:

用守恒量压缩维数。 Heisenberg 哈密顿量守恒总磁化 $S^z_\text{tot} = \sum_i S_i^z$。为什么守恒？逐项检查：

  • Diagonal term $S_i^z S_{i+1}^z$: this only measures the spins without changing any site's $S^z$ value, so it naturally commutes with $S^z_\text{tot}$.
  • Flip term $S_i^+ S_{i+1}^-$: this flips site $i$ from down to up ($+1/2$) while simultaneously flipping site $i+1$ from up to down ($-1/2$), giving a net change of zero.
  • 对角项 $S_i^z S_{i+1}^z$：只测量自旋，不改变任何格点的 $S^z$ 值，自然与 $S^z_\text{tot}$ 对易。
  • 翻转项 $S_i^+ S_{i+1}^-$：把格点 $i$ 的自旋从下翻到上（$+1/2$），同时把格点 $i+1$ 的自旋从上翻到下（$-1/2$），净变化为零。

More compactly, using the commutation relation $[S^z, S^\pm] = \pm S^\pm$:

用对易关系写得更紧凑。利用 $[S^z, S^\pm] = \pm S^\pm$：

$$[S_i^+ S_{i+1}^-,\, S^z_\text{tot}] = S_i^+ \underbrace{[S_{i+1}^-,\, S^z_{i+1}]}_{+S_{i+1}^-} + \underbrace{[S_i^+,\, S^z_i]}_{-S_i^+} S_{i+1}^- = S_i^+ S_{i+1}^- - S_i^+ S_{i+1}^- = 0$$

Therefore $[H, S^z_\text{tot}] = 0$: the Hamiltonian is block-diagonal, with identically zero matrix elements between different $S^z_\text{tot}$ sectors. We only need to diagonalize the submatrix within each sector, without dealing with the full Hilbert space.

因此 $[H, S^z_\text{tot}] = 0$：哈密顿量是块对角的，不同 $S^z_\text{tot}$ 扇区之间矩阵元恒为零。我们只需对角化各扇区的子矩阵，无需处理全 Hilbert 空间。

For the even-$L$, zero-field, antiferromagnetic Heisenberg chain considered here, the ground state lies in the $S^z_\text{tot} = 0$ sector (exactly $L/2$ spins up) [Lieb & Mattis 1962]. The dimension of this sector is

对本文考虑的偶数 $L$、无外场、反铁磁 Heisenberg 链，基态位于 $S^z_\text{tot} = 0$ 扇区（恰好 $L/2$ 个自旋向上）[Lieb & Mattis 1962]。该扇区维数为

$$D = \binom{L}{L/2}$$

This is much smaller than the full Hilbert space $2^L$: for $L=16$, $2^{16} = 65536$ while $\binom{16}{8} = 12870$, a reduction of about 5-fold; for $L=24$, $2^{24} \approx 1.7 \times 10^7$ while $\binom{24}{12} \approx 2.7 \times 10^6$, a reduction of about 6-fold.

这比全 Hilbert 空间 $2^L$ 小得多：$L=16$ 时 $2^{16} = 65536$，$\binom{16}{8} = 12870$，降低约 5 倍；$L=24$ 时 $2^{24} \approx 1.7 \times 10^7$，$\binom{24}{12} \approx 2.7 \times 10^6$，降低约 6 倍。

Constructing the basis states: We use an $L$-bit integer s to encode a single configuration (basis state) of the spin chain. By convention, bit $i$ (counting from the least significant bit) represents the spin at site $i$: bit 1 means “up,” bit 0 means “down.” The number of up-spins is then the number of 1s in the binary representation of this integer (also called the popcount).

构造基矢：用一个 $L$ 位二进制整数 s 来编码一条自旋链的一个构型（basis state）。约定第 $i$ 位（从低位开始数）表示格点 $i$ 的自旋：位为 1 表示"向上"，位为 0 表示"向下"。那么"向上自旋数"就是这个整数的二进制展开里 1 的个数（也叫 popcount）。

The following function scans all $2^L$ possible $L$-bit integers and selects those whose popcount equals nup (i.e., the basis states of a fixed $S^z_\text{tot}$ sector), collecting them into a NumPy array:

下面这段函数做的事是：在所有 $2^L$ 个 $L$ 位整数里，筛选出 popcount 恰好等于 nup 的那些（也就是固定 $S^z_\text{tot}$ 扇区的基矢），并把它们放进一个 NumPy 数组里： 

def build_basis(L, nup):
    """All L-bit integers with popcount == nup (Sz sector basis)."""
    return np.array([s for s in range(1 << L)
                     if bin(s).count("1") == nup],
                    dtype=np.int64)

A few supplementary notes (for readers encountering these Python/NumPy constructs for the first time):

几点补充说明（面向第一次见到这些 Python/NumPy 语法的读者）：

  • What is 1 << L: << is the bitwise left shift operator. 1 << L shifts the binary 1 left by $L$ positions, yielding $2^L$. Thus range(1 << L) enumerates all integers corresponding to $L$-bit binary strings (from 0 to 2^L-1).
  • What is bin(s): bin is a Python built-in that converts an integer to its binary string representation with the prefix "0b". For example, bin(6) returns "0b110".
  • What is .count("1"): This is a string method that counts occurrences of a substring; for "0b110", count("1") == 2. Therefore bin(s).count("1") gives the popcount.
  • List comprehension [s for s in ... if ...]: Read as “iterate over each s in ..., and collect it if the if condition is satisfied.”
  • np.array(..., dtype=np.int64): Converts a Python list to a NumPy array; dtype=np.int64 specifies 64-bit integer storage.
  • 1 << L 是什么：<< 是按位左移运算，1 << L 表示把二进制的 1 向左移动 $L$ 位，结果就是 $2^L$。因此 range(1 << L) 正好枚举所有 $L$ 位二进制串对应的整数（从 0 到 2^L-1）。
  • bin(s) 是什么：bin 是 Python 内置函数，把整数转成二进制的"字符串表示"，并带前缀 "0b"。例如 bin(6) 得到 "0b110"。
  • .count("1") 是什么：这是字符串的方法，统计某个子串出现次数；对 "0b110" 来说，count("1") == 2。因此 bin(s).count("1") 就是 popcount。
  • 列表推导式 [s for s in ... if ...]：读作"遍历 ... 里的每个 s，只要满足 if 条件就收集起来"。
  • np.array(..., dtype=np.int64)：把 Python 列表转成 NumPy 数组；dtype=np.int64 指定用 64 位整数存储。

The return value is a sorted integer array: since range(1<<L) itself iterates in ascending order, the filtered values of s remain in ascending order. This ordering is crucial — it enables binary search to quickly map “state (integer) → index in basis” later on.

返回值是排好序的整数数组：因为 range(1<<L) 本身就是从小到大遍历，筛选后保留下来的 s 仍然保持升序。这个"有序性"很重要，后面才能用二分查找把"态（整数）→ 在 basis 里的下标"快速定位到。

1.3 Binary Search: State to Index 1.3 二分查找：态 → 索引

When constructing the Hamiltonian matrix, we frequently need to answer this question: the Hamiltonian transforms basis state $|s\rangle$ into another state $|s'\rangle$ — which column of the matrix does $|s'\rangle$ correspond to?

构建 Hamiltonian 矩阵时，我们需要频繁回答这样一个问题：哈密顿量把基矢 $|s\rangle$ 变换到另一个态 $|s'\rangle$，$|s'\rangle$ 对应矩阵的哪一列？

The answer is simple: look up its position in the basis array. Since the basis is a sorted integer array, this is a standard binary search: compare with the middle element each time, eliminate half the candidates, with time complexity $O(\log D)$:

答案很简单：查它在基矢数组中的位置。由于基矢是排序整数数组，这是标准的二分查找（binary search）：每次比较中间元素，排除一半候选，时间复杂度 $O(\log D)$： 

from bisect import bisect_left

def find_state(basis, state):
    """Binary search: index of state in sorted basis array."""
    return bisect_left(basis, state)

For the system sizes in this chapter ($D \lesssim 10^6$), binary search is fast enough. Later we will introduce the combinatorial number system, which reduces the lookup time to $O(1)$, but that is an optimization issue — for now, let us get the entire pipeline running first.

对于本章的规模（$D \lesssim 10^6$），二分查找已经足够快。以后会介绍组合数系统，可以把查找时间降到 $O(1)$，但那是优化问题，现在先把整个流程跑通。

1.4 Constructing the Sparse Hamiltonian 1.4 构造稀疏 Hamiltonian

Dense vs. Sparse Matrices 稠密矩阵与稀疏矩阵

First, a basic question: how do we store the matrix?

先解释一个基本问题：矩阵怎么存？

Dense matrix: allocate a $D \times D$ two-dimensional array where every entry occupies memory, regardless of whether it is nonzero. For $D = 12870$ ($L=16$), a dense matrix requires $12870^2 \times 8$ bytes $\approx 1.3$ GB; for $D = 2.7 \times 10^6$ ($L=24$), it would require about $58$ TB — impossible to fit in memory.

稠密矩阵（dense matrix）：开一个 $D \times D$ 的二维数组，不管有没有非零元，每个位置都占内存。$D = 12870$（$L=16$）时，稠密矩阵需要 $12870^2 \times 8$ 字节 $\approx 1.3$ GB，$D = 2.7 \times 10^6$（$L=24$）时需要约 $58$ TB——根本放不进内存。

Sparse matrix: store only the nonzero entries. The two most common formats are:

稀疏矩阵（sparse matrix）：只存非零元。最常用的两种格式是：

COO (Coordinate format): three arrays store the row indices, column indices, and values respectively. During construction, one simply appends — very convenient.

COO（Coordinate format）：用三个数组分别存行下标、列下标、数值。构建时只需 append，非常方便。

CSR (Compressed Sparse Row): groups the nonzero entries of each row together in contiguous storage, making matrix-vector multiplication efficient (the inner loop of the Lanczos algorithm is a matrix-vector multiplication). A small 4×4 example:

CSR（Compressed Sparse Row）：把同一行的非零元压缩在一起，存储连续，矩阵-向量乘法效率高（Lanczos 算法的内层循环就是矩阵-向量乘法）。举个 4×4 的小例子：

$$A=\begin{pmatrix} 10 & 0 & 0 & 2\\ 3 & 9 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 7 & 8 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$$

CSR represents it with three one-dimensional arrays:

  • data = [10, 2, 3, 9, 7, 8, 4] (nonzero values in row-major order)
  • indices = [0, 3, 0, 1, 1, 2, 3] (column index for each nonzero value)
  • indptr = [0, 2, 4, 6, 7] (the nonzero entries of row $i$ are the slice data[indptr[i]:indptr[i+1]])

For matrix-vector multiplication $y=Ax$, one can scan row by row:

CSR 用三个一维数组表示它：

  • data = [10, 2, 3, 9, 7, 8, 4]（按"行优先"依次存非零值）
  • indices = [0, 3, 0, 1, 1, 2, 3]（每个非零值对应的列号）
  • indptr = [0, 2, 4, 6, 7]（第 $i$ 行的非零元在 data 里的切片范围是 data[indptr[i]:indptr[i+1]]）

这样做矩阵-向量乘法 $y=Ax$ 时，可以逐行扫：

$$y_i=\sum_{k=\text{indptr}[i]}^{\text{indptr}[i+1]-1} \text{data}[k]\; x_{\text{indices}[k]}$$

The nonzero entries of each row are contiguous in memory, so CPU access is more sequential — this makes CSR better suited than COO or dense matrices for repeated multiplications.

每一行的非零元在内存里是连续的（data/indices 连续切片），CPU 访问更"顺"，因此比 COO/稠密矩阵更适合反复做乘法。

Why COO first, then convert to CSR? During Hamiltonian construction, we iterate over basis states and append new nonzero entries on the fly — COO's list operations are simple and efficient. After construction, we convert to CSR in one pass, for the Lanczos solver to use repeatedly.

为什么先 COO 再转 CSR？ 构建哈密顿量时，我们逐个遍历基矢，随时 append 新的非零元——COO 的列表操作简单高效。构建完成后一次性转为 CSR，供 Lanczos 求解器反复使用。

How sparse is the Heisenberg matrix? Each basis state $|s\rangle$ produces at most $L$ flip terms plus one diagonal entry, giving roughly $L+1$ nonzero entries. For $L=16$, the matrix has $12870^2 \approx 1.7 \times 10^8$ entries in total, but only about $12870 \times 17 \approx 2 \times 10^5$ nonzero entries — a nonzero density of about $0.1\%$.

Heisenberg 矩阵有多稀疏？每个基矢 $|s\rangle$ 最多产生 $L$ 个翻转（每对相邻反平行格点贡献一个非零元），加上一个对角元，共约 $L+1$ 个非零元。$L=16$ 时矩阵共 $12870^2 \approx 1.7 \times 10^8$ 个位置，但非零元只有约 $12870 \times 17 \approx 2 \times 10^5$，稀疏度约 $0.1\%$。

Computing Matrix Elements 矩阵元的计算

The Heisenberg Hamiltonian has two types of terms:

Heisenberg 哈密顿量分两类项：

Diagonal term $S_i^z S_{i+1}^z$: depends only on the current state, changes no bits, and contributes only to the diagonal matrix element. The spin value at site $i$ is $s_i = (s \gg i)\ \&\ 1$ (read bit $i$), giving

对角项 $S_i^z S_{i+1}^z$：只依赖当前态，不改变任何比特，只贡献对角矩阵元。格点 $i$ 的自旋值 $s_i = (s \gg i)\ \&\ 1$（读第 $i$ 位），则

$$\langle s | S_i^z S_{i+1}^z | s \rangle = \left(s_i - \tfrac{1}{2}\right)\left(s_{i+1} - \tfrac{1}{2}\right) = \begin{cases} +1/4 & s_i = s_{i+1} \\ -1/4 & s_i \neq s_{i+1} \end{cases}$$

Off-diagonal term $\frac{1}{2}(S_i^+ S_{i+1}^- + S_i^- S_{i+1}^+)$: acts only on antiparallel spin pairs. For an antiparallel pair, the operator swaps the spins at these two sites. In the integer encoding, this amounts to simultaneously flipping bits $i$ and $j=(i+1)\bmod L$:

非对角项 $\frac{1}{2}(S_i^+ S_{i+1}^- + S_i^- S_{i+1}^+)$：只对反平行自旋对起作用。对反平行的一对，算符把这两个格点的自旋互换。在"整数编码"的语言里，这等价于同时翻转第 $i$ 位与第 $j=(i+1)\bmod L$ 位：

The standard way to implement “flip two bits simultaneously” is to first construct a mask that has 1s at positions $i$ and $j$ and 0s elsewhere:

实现"同时翻转两位"的标准做法是先构造一个掩码 mask，让它在第 $i$ 位和第 $j$ 位为 1，其余位为 0：

$$\text{mask} = 2^i \,|\, 2^j$$

Then use bitwise XOR to flip these two bits:

然后用按位异或（XOR）把这两位翻转：

$$|s'\rangle = \bigl|s \oplus \underbrace{(2^i \,|\, 2^j)}_{\text{mask}}\bigr\rangle$$

Example: Take $L=4$ and flip the adjacent sites $i=1$ and $j=2$. Then $2^1 = 0010_2$, $2^2 = 0100_2$, $\text{mask} = 0110_2$. Suppose $s = 0101_2$ (bit 1 is 0, bit 2 is 1 — antiparallel). Flipping: $s' = 0101_2 \oplus 0110_2 = 0011_2$. The off-diagonal matrix element has coefficient $1/2$ (times $J$).

例子：取 $L=4$，翻转相邻的 $i=1$ 和 $j=2$ 两个格点。此时 $2^1 = 0010_2$，$2^2 = 0100_2$，$\text{mask} = 0110_2$。假设 $s = 0101_2$（第 1 位是 0、第 2 位是 1，正好反平行）。翻转：$s' = 0101_2 \oplus 0110_2 = 0011_2$。对于反平行对，非对角矩阵元的系数是 $1/2$（再乘 $J$）。

Complete Code 完整代码 

def build_hamiltonian(L, basis, J=1.0):
    """Sparse Heisenberg Hamiltonian in the given Sz sector."""
    D = len(basis)
    rows, cols, vals = [], [], []

    for a, s in enumerate(basis):
        diag = 0.0
        for i in range(L):
            j = (i + 1) % L
            si = (s >> i) & 1
            sj = (s >> j) & 1

            diag += J * (si - 0.5) * (sj - 0.5)

            if si != sj:
                t = s ^ ((1 << i) | (1 << j))
                b = find_state(basis, t)
                rows.append(a); cols.append(b); vals.append(0.5 * J)

        rows.append(a); cols.append(a); vals.append(diag)

    return csr_matrix((vals, (rows, cols)), shape=(D, D))

1.5 Diagonalization: Full Matrix vs. Lanczos 1.5 对角化：全矩阵 vs Lanczos

With the sparse matrix in hand, the next step is to find the lowest eigenvalue. nanoED automatically selects the method based on the matrix dimension $D$:

有了稀疏矩阵，下一步是求最低本征值。nanoED 根据矩阵维数 $D$ 自动选择方法：

FULL_DIAG_MAX = 500

def solve(H, k=1):
    """Lowest k eigenvalues/vectors."""
    D = H.shape[0]
    if D <= FULL_DIAG_MAX:
        vals, vecs = np.linalg.eigh(H.toarray())
        return vals[:k], vecs[:, :k], "full"
    vals, vecs = eigsh(H, k=k, which="SA")
    return vals, vecs, "lanczos"

Full diagonalization (np.linalg.eigh): np refers to NumPy. Under the hood it typically calls LAPACK. The approach converts the sparse matrix to a dense matrix, then uses an $O(D^3)$ algorithm to obtain all $D$ eigenvalues and eigenvectors at once. Suitable for small systems ($D \lesssim 500$).

全对角化（np.linalg.eigh）：np 指 NumPy。底层通常调用 LAPACK。做法是先把稀疏矩阵转成稠密矩阵，再用 $O(D^3)$ 的算法一次性得到所有 $D$ 个本征值和本征向量。适合小系统（$D \lesssim 500$）。

Lanczos algorithm (scipy.sparse.linalg.eigsh): Lanczos is an iterative eigenvalue algorithm for large sparse matrices. It progressively approximates eigenstates within a Krylov subspace, where the main cost of each step is a single matrix-vector multiplication $H|\psi\rangle$. In SciPy, eigsh is typically implemented using ARPACK.

Lanczos 算法（scipy.sparse.linalg.eigsh）：Lanczos 是求解大型稀疏矩阵的迭代特征值算法：它在 Krylov 子空间里逐步逼近本征态，每一步的主要开销就是一次矩阵-向量乘法 $H|\psi\rangle$。在 SciPy 里，eigsh 的实现通常基于 ARPACK。

For a sparse matrix, a single matrix-vector multiplication costs $O(\text{nnz}) \approx O(LD)$; after roughly $m = 50$–$200$ iterations the lowest few eigenvalues converge, giving a total cost of $O(mLD)$, far faster than $O(D^3)$.

对稀疏矩阵，一次矩阵-向量乘法的代价为 $O(\text{nnz}) \approx O(LD)$；经过约 $m = 50\text{–}200$ 步迭代后收敛到最低几个本征值，总代价 $O(mLD)$，远快于 $O(D^3)$。

1.6 Finite-Size Scaling 1.6 有限尺寸标度

ED works on finite systems; one must use finite-size scaling to extrapolate to the thermodynamic limit. For the Heisenberg chain, the ground-state energy is known to satisfy

ED 在有限系统上工作，必须通过有限尺寸标度（finite-size scaling）外推到热力学极限。对 Heisenberg 链，已知基态能量满足

$$\frac{E_0}{L} = e_\infty + \frac{a}{L^2} + \cdots$$

The leading correction is $1/L^2$, arising from the quantum zero-point energy of long-wavelength spin fluctuations; a rigorous derivation requires conformal field theory — the low-energy effective theory of the Heisenberg chain is the SU(2)$_1$ WZW model with central charge $c=1$ [Blöte, Cardy & Nightingale 1986; Affleck 1986].

主导修正为 $1/L^2$，来自长波自旋涨落的量子零点能；严格推导需要共形场论——Heisenberg 链的低能有效理论是中心荷 $c=1$ 的 SU(2)₁ WZW 模型 [Blöte, Cardy & Nightingale 1986; Affleck 1986]。

1.7 Where Is the Bottleneck? 1.7 瓶颈在哪里？

Running benchmark.py scans $L = 8, 10, \ldots, 24$, decomposing the runtime at each system size into three phases: build_basis, build_hamiltonian, and solve. Figure 2 shows the results.

运行 benchmark.py 扫描 $L = 8, 10, \ldots, 24$，将每个系统尺寸的运行时间分解为三个阶段：build_basis、build_hamiltonian、solve。图 2 展示了结果。

Several key observations:

几个关键观察：

Hamiltonian construction is the bottleneck. Although solve is the theoretically most complex step, in the actual code build_hamiltonian's pure Python double loop ($D \times L$ iterations) is the slowest part. At $L=24$, build_hamiltonian accounts for about 92% of the total time, while Lanczos solve takes only about 7%.

构建 Hamiltonian 是瓶颈。 虽然 solve 是理论上最复杂的步骤，但在实际代码中，build_hamiltonian 的纯 Python 双重循环（$D \times L$ 次迭代）才是最慢的部分。$L=24$ 时 build_hamiltonian 占总时间的约 92%，而 Lanczos solve 只占约 7%。

Full diagonalization vs. Lanczos. $L = 8$ ($D = 70$) and $L = 10$ ($D = 252$) use full diagonalization since $D \leq 500$; $L \geq 12$ switches to Lanczos. Notably, the solve time for $L=10$ is actually higher than for $L=12$: full diagonalization requires all $D$ eigenvalues ($O(D^3)$), while Lanczos needs only $k=1$, converging in roughly 50 sparse matrix-vector multiplications.

全对角化 vs Lanczos。 $L = 8$（$D = 70$）和 $L = 10$（$D = 252$）因 $D \leq 500$ 使用全对角化；$L \geq 12$ 切换到 Lanczos。值得注意的是 $L=10$ 的 solve 时间反而高于 $L=12$：全对角化要求全部 $D$ 个本征值（$O(D^3)$），而 Lanczos 只求 $k=1$ 个，仅需约 50 次稀疏矩阵-向量乘法即可收敛。

The exponential wall. Every increase of $L$ by 2 multiplies $D$ by roughly 5–10, and the total time explodes accordingly: $L=20$ takes about 6s, $L=22$ about 28s, $L=24$ about 121s.

指数墙。 $L$ 每增加 2，$D$ 约增加 5–10 倍，总时间也随之爆炸式增长：$L=20$ 约 6s，$L=22$ 约 28s，$L=24$ 约 121s。

Summary 小结

This chapter presented the minimal complete implementation of ED. Four key design decisions:

本章展示了 ED 的最小完整实现。四个关键设计决策：

  1. Binary encoding: spin state = integer, bit operations = physical operators, with virtually zero overhead
  2. $S^z$ conservation: popcount filtering reduces the dimension from $2^L$ to $\binom{L}{L/2}$, a compression of about 6-fold ($L=24$)
  3. Sparse storage: only $O(L)$ nonzero entries per row, reducing memory from $O(D^2)$ to $O(LD)$
  4. Lanczos: $O(mLD)$ instead of $O(D^3)$; at $L=24$ the solve takes only about 10s
  1. 二进制编码：自旋态 = 整数，位操作 = 物理算符，几乎零额外开销
  2. $S^z$ 守恒：popcount 筛选，维数从 $2^L$ 降到 $\binom{L}{L/2}$，约压缩 6 倍（$L=24$）
  3. 稀疏存储：每行只有 $O(L)$ 个非零元，内存从 $O(D^2)$ 降到 $O(LD)$
  4. Lanczos：$O(mLD)$ 而非 $O(D^3)$，$L=24$ 时 solve 只需约 10s

The performance analysis also points toward next steps:

性能分析也揭示了下一步的方向：

  • Python loops are the real bottleneck; Cython/Numba can provide 100x speedup
  • Symmetries (translation, reflection) can further reduce the dimension by roughly $2L$-fold
  • Basis indexing: the $O(\log D)$ binary search can be reduced to $O(1)$ using the combinatorial number system
  • Python 循环是真正的瓶颈，以后介绍 Cython/Numba 可加速 100 倍
  • 对称性（平移、反射）可将维数再降约 $2L$ 倍，以后介绍
  • 基矢索引：二分查找的 $O(\log D)$ 可用组合数系统降到 $O(1)$，以后介绍
